freeyourstuff.cc

Change the rules.

We all contribute to websites that ask us for our reviews, our opinions, our creative work. They monetize our content while nobody else can use it. New websites have a hard time, even when they are more ethical, more open, or more community-minded. The rich get richer. We think the web deserves better.

We are asking you to join us, and "free your stuff". You can make sure your work lives forever. You can give everyone who wants to build a better web a head start. All you need is our browser extension, and this website.
